Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the official website of the First Ukrainian Baptist Church Band (FUBC Band). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors unless it is voluntarily provided. The only data we may process includes:

  • Attendance Reports: Names and dates submitted via our "Report Absence" form.
  • Anonymous Analytics: Basic site traffic data (if applicable) to monitor performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information submitted through forms is used strictly for internal band coordination (e.g., tracking rehearsal attendance). We do not sell, trade, or rent user information to others.

4. Third-Party Services

This site may link to third-party content (e.g., YouTube for media). These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at fubcband@gmail.com.
