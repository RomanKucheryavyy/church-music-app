Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This website is operated by the First Ukrainian Baptist Church (FUBC) Band Ministry for the purpose of scheduling, distributing music, and coordinating rehearsals. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary for the coordination of our band activities:

  • Attendance Data: When you submit an "Absence Report," we collect your Name and the Date of your planned absence.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics data to understand website traffic, but this is not linked to your personal identity.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Planning rehearsals and Sunday services.
  • Notifying conductors of musician availability.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Third-Party Services

This website uses Netlify for hosting and form processing. When you submit a form, your data is securely processed by Netlify. Please refer to Netlify's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. External Links

Our website frequently links to external third-party resources for educational and rehearsal purposes, including:

  • YouTube Videos: For listening and reference.
  • Chord Charts & Sheet Music: Hosted on external sites or provided for ministry use.

Disclaimer: We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites linked from our platform.
